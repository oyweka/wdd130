Alex Ochieng Oyweka | WDD 130

Hello, my name is Alex Ochieng Oyweka. I am a Kenyan living in Nairobi City. I am a last-born in a family of four children. I have one brother and two sisters. Our first born is a girl, and is now married. The second born is my brother and the third is my sister. They are all married. I was born in a christian family though not members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saint. My parents church, which I attended for most of my life is the Pentecostal Assemblies of God. I joined The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saint, through my younger sister who is married to a bishop of our ward. I lived in Nairobi until I completed my primary school. For secondary school, I studied in a rural town in Kakemega county. After this, I moved to Eldoret where I did my Bachelor's degree and graduated in 2015. I became interest in tech, and decided to join BYU-Pathway and pursue a degree in software development. I am excited to join this class and learn web development. I am sure I will enjoy the class. Outside classroom, I love football, where I play as a midfielder.
